Job Title: AI Engineer – Recruitment Automation
Location: Remote
Experience Level:0 years
Salary Range: ₹8–12 LPA

Job Overview:
We are seeking a skilled and motivated AI Engineer to develop an AI-powered job screening system. The role focuses on automating the recruitment process using large language models (LLMs), data intelligence, and multi-agent workflows.

Key Responsibilities:

Build AI agents to read and summarize job descriptions.

Extract relevant details from candidate CVs such as skills, education, and experience.

Match candidate profiles to job descriptions using scoring or embedding techniques.

Shortlist candidates based on defined match thresholds.

Send interview invitations via email, including scheduling details.

Maintain long-term memory of past interactions using SQLite.

Required Skills:

Proficiency in Python.

Experience with LangChain, LangGraph, or similar multi-agent AI frameworks.

Familiarity with large language models (OpenAI, Groq, Cohere, etc.).

Knowledge of SQLite or lightweight relational databases.

Ability to parse PDFs, DOCX, and structured/unstructured CV formats.

Basic experience with email sending tools (SMTP, SendGrid, etc.).

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience designing agent-based workflows.

Exposure to recruitment platforms like LinkedIn or Naukri.

Previous work in HR automation or resume screening tools.

Education:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or a related field.
